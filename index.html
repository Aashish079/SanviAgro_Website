Farm Fresh products in Kathmandu

Sanviagro is a family-run farm business located in Sindhupalcowk, Bagmati. We offer fresh, organic produce and herbs grown in our local fields, as well as homemade honey and dairy products. Our commitment to sustainability and quality ensures that our customers receive the freshest and most nutritious products. Come join us for a taste of the best the countryside has to offer!

Our Services

Crop Management Service

This service provides assistance with crop and pest management, irrigation, and fertilization to help maximize yield and profits.

Livestock Care Service

This service provides care and management for livestock such as cows, pigs, chickens, and sheep.

Landscape Maintenance Service

This service provides maintenance of farm grounds, such as mowing, weed removal, and fertilizing, to keep the land in good condition.

About Us

We specialize in growing and selling fresh fruits and vegetables, and offer a variety of other products, including grains, dairy, and other agricultural products. We are committed to providing our customers with the freshest produce possible, so we pick and process our fruits and vegetables daily. We take great pride in being able to provide our customers with the freshest and most sustainable produce available, and are always excited to help people get the most out of their shopping experience.
